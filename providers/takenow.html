TakeNow Uganda: Complete Guide (2026)

How it works • Requirements • Exact payment steps • FAQs

Written by Kayizzi Richard · Published on March 17, 2026 · Updated on

What is TakeNow Uganda?

TakeNow (also called TECNO TakeNow) is a simple hire-purchase program that lets you take home a Tecno, Infinix or Itel phone today after paying a deposit. You then repay the balance over 6 months. It runs through partner shops and is powered by MTN’s Mpola Mpola payment scheme.

How TakeNow Works (Step by Step)

1. Visit any Tecno, Infinix, Itel or MTN shop with the TakeNow sign.
2. Choose your phone and show your original National ID.
3. Pay the deposit (usually 30–40%).
4. Take the phone home the same day.
5. Repay the balance over 6 months via MTN Mpola Mpola.

Many customers also get 1GB free MTN data every month during repayment.

Requirements

  • Original National ID (only document needed)
  • Deposit (from UGX 150,000 upwards depending on the phone)
  • Must be 18 years or older

No bank statements, no guarantor, no salary slip required.

How to Make Your TakeNow Payments (Mpola Mpola) – Very Important

TakeNow repayments are done through MTN Mobile Money using the official *165*5# Mpola Mpola menu. This is the easiest and most reliable way.

Exact steps to pay:

  1. Dial *165*5# on your MTN line.
  2. Select the TakeNow/Mpola Mpola option (it will show your phone and remaining balance).
  3. Enter the amount you want to pay (daily, weekly or monthly).
  4. Confirm with your MoMo PIN.

You can also check your exact monthly repayment amount and remaining balance anytime by dialling the same *165*5#.

Extra tips:

  • Early repayment is allowed — you can finish faster with no penalty.
  • Set a mobile money reminder or use auto-pay if available.
  • If you miss a payment, contact the shop immediately to avoid penalties or repossession.

You can also pay cash at the same shop where you bought the phone if you prefer.

Benefits & Risks

Benefits: Instant ownership, flexible 6 months, free data, available countrywide, occasional 0% interest promos.

Risks to watch: Late payments attract penalties and possible repossession. Always confirm the total amount you will pay before signing.

FAQs (2026)

Can I pay early? Yes.
Is there interest? Depends on the current promotion.
What if I lose the phone? You are still responsible for full repayment.
Where is TakeNow available? Almost every Tecno/Infinix/Itel and many MTN shops nationwide.

Final Advice

TakeNow is convenient when you need a phone immediately and can comfortably afford the repayments. Before you sign, ask the shop for your exact total repayment amount and the payment schedule in writing. Knowledge before commitment!
